My top 3 movies of all-time!

ATL (2006)

Dope movie featuring T.I., Evan Ross, and more famous actors that portray the realisitc struggles of the street life in Atlanta, Georgia.

Choose Or Die (2022)

Action-packed thriller film that involves a horror game played for money that interacts with players in real life and real time.

Jackass 2 (2006)

Goofy movie filled with stunt dummies who practice stupidity at the cost of their own life and health for entertainment.
